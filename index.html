The Beginning

The Life of Xander

Once upon a time, the was a woman who lived out in the forest where the trees were thick and the animals ran wild. After nine months of waiting, she welcomed into the world a baby boy. After one look, she decided that he shall be called Xander.

His early years were spent running rampant amongst the animals. He learned how to jump and climb the trees with the monkeys. His time with the jaguars taught him how to hunt and the giant otters taught him how to swim. It was a great childhood. His mom was proud of him.
