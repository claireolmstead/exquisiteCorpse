This Is Our Exquisite Corpse

Let's see what comes of it...

The Beginning

The Life of Xander

Once upon a time, the was a woman who lived out in the forest where the trees were thick and the animals ran wild. After nine months of waiting, she welcomed into the world a baby boy. After one look, she decided that he shall be called Xander.

His early years were spent running rampant amongst the animals. He learned how to jump and climb the trees with the monkeys. His time with the jaguars taught him how to hunt and the giant otters taught him how to swim. It was a great childhood. His mom was proud of him.

►►►

The Middle

Xander's life changed when he was running through the forest one day. He was jumping around with his monkey friends when out of the corner of his eye, he saw a new plant that he immediately wanted to eat. The plant was beautiful and very tasty, and he ate as many as he could until he was full. However, thirty minutes later it felt like Xander was floating among the stars that he saw at night, and was one with the Earth.

Xander ran as fast and long as he could as it felt like he was a spaceship flying through the galaxy. Xander flew all the way to the beach, where a group of conquistadors found him and were amused by him. They decided to take him with them as their jester and he entertained them for many years.

►►►

The Other Middle

Xander had a wonderful life working as a jester for his new Spanish friends. He travelled to strange, far away places and was able to make the native people's he encountered share the joy of laughter. One day Xander arrived at a place called Boulder. There he met strange people with long pieces of wood strapped to their feet. They rode their logs down tall mountains covered in a strange white powder.

One day he met a man atop one of these mountains who he entertained with his miming and dancing. The man was grateful to meet the young Xander and offered him a glass dish filled with strange green and purple herbs. Xander took a deep breath and watched as the contents of the pot turned to black.

►►►

The End
